About us



What we do ?

Diabetes is a chronic health problem with devastating, yet preventable consequences.

It is characterized by high blood glucose levels resulting from defects in insulin production, insulin action, or both.

Regarding its cure, major challenge faced by healthcare organizations (hospitals, medical centres) is the provision of quality services at affordable costs.

To minimize the cost of clinical tests we have designed a system.

System will take information from the user and will use that and datasets by machine learning approaches to prognosticate the likelihood of diabetes in patients with maximum accuracy.

User will also have the option to consult Endocrinologist (Doctor with expertise in diabetes) using the app.
